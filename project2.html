Master Chef

Overview

The Recipe Finder project addresses the common problem of deciding what to cook by offering a web application that provides tailored recipe suggestions based on keywords, cooking time, and available ingredients. Designed to be versatile and user-friendly, the app enables users to discover recipes no matter their location, ensuring a seamless cooking experience.

Watch Our Demo

Key Features

  • Search for recipes by ingredients, cooking duration, or specific keywords.
  • View detailed nutritional breakdowns for each recipe.
  • Receive recipe suggestions tailored to user preferences and past feedback.
